Student Profile Introduction

Hi!, I’d like to introduce myself, I am Jessica Singh from ICT21 currently studying Grade-12 Senior High School at De La Salle University-Dasmariñas. 18 years old. If you see me in person don’t be shock because I’m just a small girl. I love eating and reading stories on Wattpad.

The Life of an ICT Student

Being an ICT Student is not easy because you will really need to give your time to study. It takes time to learn one lesson. And you need to balance your time and create a timeline. I as an ICT student are having a hard time because of my patience.

Advices for future ICT Students

Hi future ICT students! My advices for all of you is that first, you need to practice your patience because in this strand patience is the key to survive this strand, second, learn by practicing because I believe practice makes you learn, and third, always believe in yourself because the only person who can help you is yourself.
