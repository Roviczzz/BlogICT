The Life of an ICT Student

For me, the life of an ICT student is like the beautiful waves of the ocean, sometimes it gets stormy, sometimes it's calm, and sometimes it's still. Programming takes practice, focus, patience, hardwork, and creativity to make a masterpiece. It's like art but in codes. You type in the paint, every stroke and brush is like every code that you put in. Every mistake is just a chance of making it into a full functioning app.

Programming is a step-by-step process, each step you take will be harder and harder but a new lesson will always be learned in those steps. As the saying goes "Practice makes perfect". I like learning new things, new lessons in each mishaps come in the way.

Advices for future ICT Students

Word of advice for the future ICT students out there, just have fun in each problem you encounter, learning shouldn't be a burden but a process of testing what are you're capabilities. There will be times that will make you feel heavy and hopeless, but in those times, is an opportunity for you to get up and stand strong.
